
PM-JAY Ayushman Bharat Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) provides health coverage of Rs. 5 lakh
per family per year to economically vulnerable families listed in SECC 2011.

Eligibility: SC, ST, OBC, EWS, BPL families. No age cap. No cap on family size.
Income limit: Rs. 2.5 lakh per year.

Required Documents: Aadhaar card, Ration card, SECC registration proof
How to Apply: Visit nearest Ayushman Mitra at empanelled hospital with Aadhaar card.
Benefits: Cashless treatment up to Rs. 5 lakh per year at 25,000+ hospitals.
